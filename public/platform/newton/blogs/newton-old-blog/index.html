Welcome to Newton Mobile

Welcome to Newton Mobile

by Tom DeMaio, Newton Product Owner

Published: July 29, 2020

We’re excited to announce the launch of Newton Mobile, a reimagined platform for developing and deploying mobile apps at Pfizer

Over the past six months, the Platform DevOps Newton Mobile Team has been completely reimagining outdated and disconnected processes into a new comprehensive and robust platform offering that focuses on improving the experience of developing and deploying mobile apps at Pfizer. So today, we’re excited to announce the launch of Newton Mobile.



About the platform

Newton Mobile platform is Pfizer Digital’s solution to accelerate and simplify the development and delivery of best-in-class mobile applications and digital companions to patients, customers, healthcare providers, and colleagues across mobile and connected health ecosystems.

Our three core goals:

  1. To enable DSE’s teams to develop Digital Companions and other apps faster and better
  2. To ensure all DSE’s apps reach high levels of security and compliance
  3. To deliver the core capabilities that advance DSE’s ability to deliver customer value, including:
    • Personalized experiences
    • Integrations with Wearables / Connected Health partners / Hardware / Software
    • Integrations with Customer Data Platforms (PMAP)


The genesis of Newton Mobile:

Historically at Pfizer, mobile development occurred mostly in a waterfall manner. As project teams increasingly use agile methodologies, the legacy processes for releasing mobile apps to end-users are showing the strain. To make matters worse, when a problem occurs during a release, the resubmission process can stretch from days to weeks. The agile transformation our company is embarking on will no longer support this approach.

Our strategy in early 2020 was clear: We wanted to create a foundation on which we could continue to expand and innovate to increasingly deliver value to the teams creating mobile apps at Pfizer. We wanted to replace the difficult publishing processes and facilitate fast, easy and frequent deployments. We set to work, creating new ways of working at both the platform infrastructure and the application framework layers.

Even at the pre-release stage, the new platform and our centrally-managed CI/CD started to deliver value for our early pilot apps with new automated processes for configuring, building, and deploying the apps to our internal BetaApps distribution site.

Our automation efforts also included new centrally-managed compliance and security checks that run on each app build, directly delivering value through automated quality assurance for any quality issues found during development.

Finally, we then took the platform to the next level by making upload and delivery of the app, app store metadata, and screenshots to the Apple and Google App Store consoles automated and self-service for app teams. This had never before been possible for mobile app teams at Pfizer. With automation in place, the platform increases the velocity of development teams and the rate at which new mobile apps and app features can deliver value to end-users: We achieved this by creating fast feedback loops during development, removing common failures, rejections, delays, and production bugs in mobile platform apps.

At the application framework layer, the mobile platform has a refined standard offering. It provides a consistent and well-documented approach to rapidly developing apps for iOS and Android mobile app ecosystems.

Finally, through the use of standardized, well-tested, and reusable components, development teams can create and deploy new apps and features to their end-users more rapidly.

These components, coupled with the Pfizer Digital Helix Design System, align and synergize with other platforms and contributors to mobile app designs.

Why our work matters:

Newton Mobile reduces development and deployment pain for mobile apps at Pfizer, enabling agile teams to work in short, customer-focused iterations, rapidly delivering value to patients.

The Newton Mobile Team is committed to continual improvement of mobile development and deployment in support of Pfizer’s agile transformation.

We’ve also been working hard to align with core corporate goals, specifically Pfizer’s Bold Moves and Big Ideas, to ‘win the digital race in pharma’ and drive ‘breakthroughs that change patients’ lives.’

Benefits of Newton Mobile platform for new DSE projects include:

Cross-Platform Development and speed

A simplified development workflow empowers you to develop the best feature-rich applications for your end-users across all platforms. Additionally, by building on the latest hybrid technology, you can develop your app once from a single codebase and deploy it everywhere - including to Apple and Google Android platforms and the web. We’ve also vastly streamlined our process thanks to end-to-end build and release automation, with apps in the app store and ready for final approval in minutes.

Reusable Components

Newton Mobile offers an extensive library of reusable and pre-built Helix UI elements, enabling you to design your app quickly. App development is also faster, thanks to the growing repository of Helix-compatible components.

Ready-to-use Cloud Data Storage and APIs

Mobile apps are built-out using Newton, Pfizer’s in-house, secure, cloud-based back-end. This cloud platform offers the ease of storing and retrieving data without the need for custom back-end development by using a library of standard reusable APIs and SDKs. What’s more, Newton Mobile enables integration of apps with wearables, connected health technology, and 3rd party devices.

Enriched Messaging Experience + Social Login

Engaging and retaining users with personalized push and email notifications - plus enabling them to register securely via email and social apps - is crucial to the success of an app. This is why Newton Mobile delivers rich, contextual, timely notifications and social login capabilities to users across platforms.

Modern, Automated, Developer Workflow

Developers can continuously build and test code on Newton Mobile’s centralized, cloud-based build system, accelerating the integration of new features. We also ensure early adopters and project stakeholders have the latest build for testing from our custom BetaApps site, while our AppCheck suite of in-built code checks runs on each build to catch issues early.

Security

Security is key to Newton Mobile. Reusable components are centrally managed, maintained, and updated, while apps using our components have these dependencies automatically updated. What’s more, builds are compiled, signed, and uploaded to App Stores without modification or re-signing, and Snyk and AppScan security tools provide automated, fast feedback on risks.
