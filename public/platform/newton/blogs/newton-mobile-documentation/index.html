Serving our documentation users

Serving our documentation users

by Tom DeMaio, Newton Mobile Product Owner

Published: Dec 4, 2021

We need your help! If you use Newton Mobile’s docs, read on…

Why documentation matters

Whether you’re working with Newton for the first time or revisiting an existing project, chances are you’ll want to refer to the docs to confirm some details. Having information readily and accessibly available means users are able to self-serve, upskill and troubleshoot with confidence. Wasted time spent deducing how new capabilities function is eliminated, and it’s easier to make the most of powerful features without spending hours on trial-and-error learning.

Importantly, documentation provides access to different types of information which are relevant to users of a system or technology. It provides essential context about the landscape as well as lays groundwork for the needs and approaches of development within such a system. Good documentation also provides invaluable, centralized help in the form of FAQs, troubleshooting guides, and user guides. It provides not just the how, but also a chance to understand the why - an incredibly effective way to onboard users and have them “grok” your platform, software, product or service.

The impact of developer documentation cannot be understated

Documentation is important, but it’s often jettisoned or relegated due to time constraints. However, for developers working on an app built on a platform like Newton Mobile, good documentation impacts not only their experience, but also that of the app’s end user. The benefits of having up-to-date, clear and concise docs cascade.

What improvement looks like

Given the potential impact of good documentation, we’re pro-actively re-inventing the Newton Mobile docs. We’re consolidating all of our existing documentation across the platform into a single, central hub, while performing a comprehensive review to ensure accuracy. We’re also actively reaching out to our users - developers who use our docs - to solicit their opinions and understand their needs.

We want docs that are fit for purpose. The should meet our users’ needs: be intuitive and easy to use, with comprehensive and accurate information. To optimise usability and accessibility, we want to present our docs in the optimal format and structure for our users. And that’s where you, our users, come in! Let us know what works for you: have your say in what we produce.

We can’t do it without you. We have a short questionnaire we need developer users of Newton Mobile to complete. It should take about five minutes - you can help us by filling it out yourself, or forwarding it to someone who can.

Improvement doesn’t end here

You know the feeling you get when you start using a new piece of software and the docs are really good? So good you want to share them with people apropos of nothing and say “these docs are really good”? That’s how we want you to feel when you use the Newton Mobile docs.

This is a big, important step for Newton Mobile and also for the Platform DevOps team - and we’re excited to be taking it. We’re going to keep improving, and keep maintaining that drive for excellence and clarity. We’re proud of the work we do, and the apps we help build.
