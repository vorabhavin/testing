Overview

Edison Lite is the fastest way to deliver the most secure, performant websites at Pfizer, with the lowest total cost of ownership.

It is intended for smaller, less complex sites that do not require the complexity of a content management system - and therefore avoids the higher maintainence overhead associated with more complex systems.

Fastest Time to Market

Edison Lite prioritizes simplicity to get to market faster.

Security and Performance
Websites are published on Edison Lite as static code - there are no databases or server-side scripts involved making this the most secure of our platforms as there the common attack vectors are not available. Static code also means high performance - there is no server side processing of pages necessary before they are delivered. 

Websites are published as static code, offering the fastest possible performance for the customer with a low hosting and maintenance overhead.

    Simplicity without Limitation

    Connections with Canvas Web Builder enable you to use drag and drop tools to build and maintain your site. Integration with Stratus means you can offer complex interactions via microservices and enable common capabilities such as search and data capture.

        Scale with Confidence

        Edison Lite’s architecture can be scaled easily - your site will stay up even when faced with unexpectedly high traffic.
