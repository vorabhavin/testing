Static Site Hosting: how is this relevant to Pfizer?

by Platform DevOps

Static sites are fast, secure, affordable - and the basis of a booming industry. Tatiana Ugriumova, Edison Product Owner, explains how Edison Lite brings the best of static sites to Pfizer.

Published: Jan 14, 2021

The concept of static site hosting

Hosting a static site is the answer when you need to display the same information to all visitors of your website. A static site is carefully engineered to be highly performant without the overheads that come with other solutions. It’s perfect for smaller websites that don’t need content updated frequently.

Static doesn’t mean your site isn’t capable of dynamic, interactive functionality - you can definitely have this, using a programming language called JavaScript. It simply means your site is comprised of pre-built files (known as source files or Markup) that do not dynamically change or need to be re-generated off the back of a request (every time a visitor opens the page in their browser).

Because of this, you don’t need a server to manage any changes – you just need a way to render your static pages. The lack of database calls and a server makes static sites incredibly fast. This stack (a suite or bundle of software used to provide a specific solution) is known as the JAM stack – JavaScript, APIs (instead of server calls), and Markup. It’s an industry-leading solution and we have a totally compliant, in-house Pfizer implementation: Edison Lite.

The beauty of Edison Lite

The Edison platform provides optimized in-house web publishing for all Pfizer business needs. Our most popular offering, Edison Lite, is the fastest way to build a simple, secure, compliant static site. Instead of having to start from scratch every time the business wants to build a static site, we have a proven method and product for giving development teams the headstart.

Building an Edison Lite site offers integrations with specialist services to vastly improve user experience. Integration with Stratus Microservices provides a host of easily-added interactive functionality (like Forms or Search), while Canvas Web Builder provides drag & drop, ‘no-code’ content management. These interfaces mean your site can be maintained easily by non-technical people.

In addition, we make it easy for Pfizer project teams to update content and manage their websites. We aim to minimize deployment blockers by largely automating our Move To Production (MTP) process and providing access to in-house support.

Prime examples of use cases at Pfizer

There is a wide portfolio of options we could use to demonstrate here – any simple Pfizer brand site, plus many of the campaign microsites for the Pfizer Health Care Professional (HCP) Portal are built on Edison Lite.

On a more current note, Edison Lite also hosts all of the 70+ Pfizer COVID-19 vaccine related sites – for instance www.cvdvaccine.com, www.cvdvaccine.ca, www.cvdvaccine.cl.
