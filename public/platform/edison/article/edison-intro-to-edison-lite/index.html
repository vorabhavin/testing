Introduction to Edison Lite: in conversation with Tatiana Ugriumova

Introduction to Edison Lite

in conversation with Tatiana Ugriumova

Tatiana  Ugriumova, Product Owner for the Edison web publishing platform,  gives us an overview of Edison’s  most widely used offering for building sites: Edison Lite

Published:
Nov 17, 2020
​​​​
​​​​​​​

What’s your role at Pfizer? 

I am the Product Owner for Edison - Pfizer’s web publishing platform. 

What is Edison Lite? 

Edison Lite is one of the two main offerings available on the Edison platform. It enables teams to create simple static sites using just HTML, CSS and JavaScript.

Why is Edison Lite important to Pfizer? 

This offering allows Pfizer to create simple websites very, very quickly. They are performant and secure, and they cost very little both to build and to maintain.

How does Edison Lite fit into the bigger picture of Pfizer Digital? 

I think Edison Lite is the future of web publishing in Pfizer Digital. We’ll see a shift towards more and more of these types of JAMstack (JavaScript, API & Markup) sites, which is a general trend we’re seeing in the tech industry outside the company. Edison Lite enables teams to create sites and publish content in a really agile way, which is important to Pfizer and will help us push to win the digital race in pharma.

What problems/challenges does Edison Lite solve? 

It lets you create a simple site quickly and inexpensively. Previously, all our sites had to use our other offering (Edison Custom), which is based on a fully customizable CMS - for which you need to have a web server and a database running. A lot of our sites do not actually need such complexity as they are primarily content - images and text. Using a CMS was an overhead: it took longer, and it cost more. Edison Lite was created to meet the needs of simple sites, and because of its flexibility, is used extensively throughout Pfizer. 

What are the benefits of Edison Lite? 

Its key aspect is its simplicity. It provides solutions without being needlessly complex or cumbersome. In addition, provides a standardized approach to building and maintaining sites. This, in turn, leads to high speed of delivery, security, high maintainability, all while maintaining low costs. 

Who would use Edison Lite/who is Edison Lite aimed at? 

People who need simple, secure, highly performant and cost-efficient sites. We currently have 240 live sites and over 300 in development. We’re confident we can help you build the right site for your needs. 

What is the Edison team currently working on? 

We are working on bridging the gap between Edison Lite and Edison Custom by providing interactive functionality for Edison Lite sites via microservices, and providing a self-service user interface for creating and editing Lite sites via Canvas Web Builder.

When Edison Lite is used with Canvas, teams can use drag & drop functionality and “no code” development to build and maintain sites on Edison Lite, which reduces their dependency on development teams. I’m really looking forward to seeing people use Edison Lite in this way.

What are some examples of things that have been successfully built on Edison Lite?

  • One of our most visited sites (www.xeljanz.com) is hugely successful - over Q3 2020, the site generated 5,448,247 views by 3,009,694 unique visitors 

  • The fastest site we ever delivered (www.maakhetvaccin.be) was two weeks from the business getting in touch with Pfizer Digital to site being live 

We are also working on multiple Lite sites for the COVID vaccine 

What makes you work successfully as a team? 

We have a very tight knit team, who have been working full time on the platform for a number of years. We respect and trust each other, and always have each other’s backs.

Being fully distributed (our team members spread across four continents and eight time zones) we put a lot of effort into staying connected, getting to know everyone and having fun together. I am really looking forward to seeing everyone in person again! 

How can people reach you and learn more about Edison Lite? 

Visit our website (www.edison.pfizer), send me an email, or find me on Teams! 

Tatiana can be contacted on Tatiana.Ugriumova@pfizer.com or via Teams.
