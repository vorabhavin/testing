'Stratus and Platform DevOps shine when we have a challenge': Stratus monthly roundup​​​​​​​

Stratus and Platform Stratus monthly roundup Feb 2021

by Jamie Hook, Stratus Platform Lead

Published: Mar 1, 2021

Distributing a pre-release build of your app to stakeholders on Apple’s TestFlight can now be done in a few simple steps as a self-service process

This is my first monthly round up, a time to reflect on what my team has achieved over the past month. Blink and you miss it. At the end of every month, I am amazed we are there already, time certainly stands still for no man. I think perhaps the rate of change, busy work schedules, home schooling and personal projects all add to the accelerated feeling.

The Stratus team is more than just the Stratus platform. Stratus provides the infrastructure services across Platform DevOps, a dedicated Microservices team, operating Smart Capture, building “Trust” (our insights platform) and much more.

February marks a milestone in our team’s growth: we are now 100 strong including all of our team members on projects like Canvas and Global Registration and Validation (GRV).

One of the major initiatives that Stratus led this month was working to resolve performance related issues on Canvas. This required collaboration across all aspects of my team: Eric Silva scaling the infrastructure, Jon Hopkins generating Canvas-specific alerting, Al Nutile and Rahul Bhatt creating production automated tests, and Luis Dalmolin, Billy Jones, Jason Stanbery, Nate Swart and many other team members from Canvas planning out the future architectural changes needed to support the growth Canvas is experiencing.

Stratus and Platform DevOps shine when we have a challenge - everyone steps up and focuses on the big picture. It’s times like these that make me proud of our team and what we can achieve.

Until next month,

Jamie
