''Lots of exciting new initiatives are kicking off utilizing Stratus': Stratus monthly roundup

Stratus monthly roundup March  2021

by Jamie Hook, Stratus Platform Lead

Published: Apr 22, 2021

Reflecting on the challenges, teamwork and achievements from Pfizer Digital’s custom application development platform

In my corner of England, spring has sprung. We have nice weather and are finally able to enjoy the outdoors a little more.

Whilst all the buds are starting to sprout, it seems our projects have had the same idea: Lots of exciting new initiatives are kicking off utilizing Stratus such as Mission Control 2, COVID-related Microservice enhancements, and many more.

The big focus for Stratus at the moment is an overhaul of the UI/UX. We are developing a card-based design system enabling us to rapidly deploy new features with a standardized UX. The team is using Build-Measure-Learn methodology to gather feedback at each design iteration.

Since my last update we have posted at least five new articles to the Stratus Docs site, including:

  • The Straw that Broke the Developer’s Back
  • Stratus Platform is More Than Just a Technology Platform - One Year Later

Plus, many updates to documentation across the board.
