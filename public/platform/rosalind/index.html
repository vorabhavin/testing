Rosalind is Pfizer's platform for personalized campaign management and development. It enables you to combine data from sources across Pfizer and beyond, orchestrate customer journeys and deliver carefully targeted campaigns.

Unify

Customer Data Management is a key capability for successful personalized campaigns. Rosalind Unify can combine Pfizer and third party data sets in a secure and compliant way giving you a powerful data source on which to base your campaigns.

Define

Rosalind Define allows you to create complex segmentation groups so that your campaigns can be highly targeted and personalized. Journey orchestration capabilities allow you to predict the next best step for any customer.

  • Journey Orchestration

Deploy

Rosalind Deploy powers campaign delivery - from email to push notifications with full compliance and management of unsubscribes and opt-outs. Integration across other Pfizer platforms ensures any actions triggered during campaigns are effectively captured.
